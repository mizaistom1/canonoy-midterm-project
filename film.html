Movie: The Proposal (2009)

A romantic comedy starring Sandra Bullock and Ryan Reynolds. When a high-powered book editor faces deportation, she convinces her assistant to marry her in a fake engagement. As they travel to his hometown to keep up the act, unexpected romance and hilarity ensue.

Movie: Parasite (2019)

A gripping South Korean thriller by Bong Joon-ho that explores class struggles. A poor family cons their way into the lives of a wealthy household, leading to unexpected twists and dark consequences. Mixing suspense, social commentary, and humor, Parasite won the Academy Award for Best Picture.
