Successfully developed an application

One of my biggest achievements while studying programming is that me and my group Successfully developed a fully-functional application. A budget planner/calculator. This budget planner helps users manage their financial struggles. In this application, users can either list down their current savings as well as their needs and wants. This application shows how much the users should spend in their savings account. This application also shows a visual representation of how much the user has spent in a month or in a day. All the user has to do is input their spendings as well as their earnings and the application will do the math for how much the user has spent or earned. My group and I have successfully defended this application with high remarks in Senior High School (SHS). The panels expressed high praise for our application. Our group was also selected as one of the representatives of our SHS strand to present our research to compete with other SHS strands. This is really one of my biggest achievements while studying so far.
